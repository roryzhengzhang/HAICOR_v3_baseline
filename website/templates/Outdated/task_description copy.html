Study introduction

The primary goal of this study is to test the usability of basline human-AI collborative reasoning system and investigate user behavior with this system. In this study, you will help AI to improve his commonsense reasoning on a story understanding question so that the modified reasoning will sound more rational to you. Our system provides a basic user interface that allows you to modify the AI reasoning with graphical manipulations, you will be asked to provide feedback about the usability in a post-study survey. The entire study will take 15-20 mins.

What's AI reasoning for?

In this study, our AI intends to reason about the fundamental human needs underlying a character's behavior in a story. For example, when see the following story:

Tom was driving his car.
He wanted to take a scenic way home.
He deliberately passed his exit.
Tom saw many beautiful trees.
Tom took the scenic way home.
Question: What's human need that drives Tom to take the scenic way home?

Our AI will predict a human need out of a set of candidate answer. Meanwhile, he will present you with a reasoning to justify his choice. For example, in the above case, he may predict "calm" as the fundamental human need behind Tom's choice of driving route (please click "human needs" button on banner to see the whole set of human needs). Plus, he may justify his choice as:

home related to rest related to calm

Here bold parts are concepts, and Italic parts are the relations between concepts (please click "relation interpretation" button on banner to see the interprtation of relations in natural language). It is likely that the prediction and rationality of his reasoning may seem flimsy to you.

Your job is to improve his flawed reasoning by (1) deleting inappropriate concepts, (2) replacing inappropriate concepts with proper ones or (3) adding new concepts so that the resulting reasoning will be more rational from your point of view.
