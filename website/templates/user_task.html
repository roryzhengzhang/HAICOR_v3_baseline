Your task

In the next page, you will be shown a story, AI human need prediction and several reasoning that AI made. Please choose one of the AI reasoning to modify (i.e. replace, add, delete nodes/edges) so that the modified reasoning can lead to a proper human need and the reasoning itself will sound more rational to you.

Example:

In the prior story example:

Tom was driving his car.
He wanted to take a scenic way home.
He deliberately passed his exit.
Tom saw many beautiful trees.
Tom took the scenic way home.
Question: What's human need that drives Tom to take the scenic way home?

AI makes a reasoning:

home related to rest related to calm

One possible modification is:

scenic related to peace related to rest

Note that if you find the original reasoning is totally nonsensical, you can create a rational one from scratch. Besides, the defualt relation type of newly created edge is "related to", you can change it with "modify edge" operation if finding the default type is improper. For relation type, it is preferable to use the offered types, but self-created types are allowed if you don't find proper types to use.
