Your task

In the next page, you will be shown (1) a pair of story and question, (2) answer predicted by AI and (3) a set of reasoning that AI draws to justify his answer. Please choose one of the AI reasoning to modify (i.e. replace, add, delete nodes/edges) so that the modified reasoning can demonstrate AI how to draw a rational reasoning from story context to a plausible answer in the given case.

Example:

In the prior story example:

Tom was driving his car.
He wanted to take a scenic way home.
He deliberately passed his exit.
Tom saw many beautiful trees.
Tom took the scenic way home.
Question: What's human need that drives Tom to take the scenic way home?

AI makes a reasoning:

home related to rest related to calm

One possible modification is:

scenic related to peace related to rest

Note that if you find the original reasoning is totally nonsensical, you can create a rational one from scratch. Besides, the defualt relation type of newly created edge is "related to", you can change the type with "modify edge" operation. For relation type, it is preferable to use the offered types, but self-created types are allowable if you don't find proper candidate types to use.
